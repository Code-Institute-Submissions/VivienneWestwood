Vivienne


Hailing from Tintwistle in Chesire, Vivienne is one of the unconventional, top fashion deigners and successful business women around. Vivienne didn't have much growing up and achieved all she did through perserverance and hard work.

In 1962, Vivienne met Malcolm McLaren of the band, the Sex Pistols. Vivienne made the clothes McLaren designed initially.

Known not to shy away from politics, Vivienne likes to have her say on causes such as Promoting Ethical Clothing, Provoking People with Punk & Protesting at the Paralympics.
